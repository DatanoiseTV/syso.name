Custom Hardware & Embedded Systems Services

I believe your projects deserve more than 'good enough.' My purpose is to deliver hardware that's not just functional, but precision-engineered to amplify your specific goals. Through Datanoise UG (haftungsbeschränkt) in Berlin, we provide end-to-end custom hardware development, turning complex challenges into bespoke, high-performance IoT and electronics solutions:

Custom Circuit Design

Bespoke electronic circuit design optimized for your specific application requirements. From concept to schematic design for clients in Berlin and globally, I create robust designs that balance performance, cost, and manufacturability.

PCB Layout & Design Services

Professional PCB layout services with attention to signal integrity, EMI/EMC compliance, and DFM. My PCB designs are optimized for both performance and manufacturability.

Embedded Firmware Development

Custom C/C++ firmware development that maximizes hardware capabilities. I create efficient, reliable, and maintainable embedded software optimized for performance and power efficiency.

Music Technology Hardware

Specialized hardware solutions for music production, audio processing, and sound generation with exceptional sonic quality. From synthesizers to effects units and controllers for the audio industry.

IoT Development & Solutions

End-to-end IoT solutions combining custom hardware, robust firmware, and scalable Go/Python backend services. I design reliable, secure IoT systems for seamless connectivity.

Production Engineering Support

Complete preparation for manufacturing, including DFM/DFA optimization, component sourcing, and assembly documentation to ensure smooth transition from prototype to production for your electronic products.
