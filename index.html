About Me

I am a seasoned IT DevOps Engineer, Embedded Systems Architect and Electronics Engineer with over a decade of expertise in designing and manufacturing custom hardware solutions. My comprehensive skill set spans the entire product development lifecycle—from initial concept and schematic design through PCB layout to manufacturing and embedded firmware implementation.

As the CEO of Datanoise UG, I specialize in creating bespoke hardware solutions for music technology and IoT applications. My company delivers end-to-end custom hardware development, from initial ideation to final manufactured product. I develop sophisticated embedded firmware in C/C++ and have extensive experience with Go and Python for tooling and backend services. My skill set extends to web and mobile development with Flutter and full-stack capabilities.

I'm particularly experienced with industrial communication protocols like CANbus, Modbus RTU/TCP, and IIO. With a strong background in system architecture and hardware security, I offer unique expertise in creating specialized custom hardware that perfectly aligns with client requirements and market demands. Whether it's musical instruments, audio processing equipment, IoT devices, or specialized control systems, I develop tailored hardware solutions that exceed expectations.
